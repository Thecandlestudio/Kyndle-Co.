Our Story

Kyndle & Co began as a small, heart-driven studio dedicated to crafting hand-poured candles that inspire comfort, calm, and creativity. Each candle is thoughtfully designed with premium wax, natural scents, and a touch of love.

We believe every space deserves warmth and every moment deserves intentionality. From cozy nights in to gifting special someone, our products are made to enhance your everyday life.

Our collections range from home décor accents and flower bouquets to scented originals and creative kits — each crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail.
